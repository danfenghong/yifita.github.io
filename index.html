Biography

I’m a PhD student in the Interactive Geometry Lab (IGL) at ETH Zurich supervised by Prof. Olga Sorkine-Hornung. Before joining IGL, I received my Bachelor degree with distinction in Electrical Engineering and Information Technology (Elektrotechnik und Informationstechnik) at Technische Universität München (TUM) and completed my Master with distinction in Robotics, System and Control at ETH Zurich.

My areas of research lies in applying machine learning techniques, especially deep learning, to challenging image and geometry processing problems.
